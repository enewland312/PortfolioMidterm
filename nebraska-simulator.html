Nebraska Medicine Training Simulator

This VR training simulator was developed for Nebraska Medicine to help healthcare professionals effectively handle infectious diseases in emergency settings. The project focused on the "Three I’s" framework: Identify, Isolate, and Inform.

  • Identify potential symptoms of infectious diseases
  • Implement proper isolation procedures
  • Communicate critical information to the appropriate medical team

The result was an engaging and educational tool that enhanced emergency response readiness through immersive virtual scenarios.
